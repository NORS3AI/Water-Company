What's inside the theme

Modular by design. Sleek by default.

Starfrost is a WooCommerce theme built around design tokens, small modular files, and zero build steps. It works out of the box and gets out of your way.

Void canvas

Pure black background with a soft radial wash. Zero decoration noise. Your products are the only thing that catches the eye.

Frost accents

An ice-cyan accent and a glacier glow. Two semantic colors do all the work — no rainbow palettes, no theme drift.

Modular files

Every concern lives in its own file under inc/. Setup, enqueue, customizer, WooCommerce, Elementor — separate and replaceable.

Design tokens

Color, type, spacing, radius, motion all live in tokens.css. Tweak one variable and the whole storefront follows.

Elementor-ready

Tokens are injected into the Elementor editor and preview iframes. Drag widgets and they pick up the brand instantly.

WooCommerce reskin

Shop archive, single product, cart, checkout, account — every customer-facing Woo surface is rebuilt in the Starfrost language.
